About the Lodge


Western Knights our objective, is to engage and inspire good men, who believe in a Supreme Being, to live according to the Masonic tenets of Friendship, Morality and Brotherly Love. Spreading the cement of brotherhood and communtiy appreciation and involvement with the objective of making the world a benevolent place.


The the purpose of this website is to provide information about the Freemasonry, Lodge activities and general information. Western Knights Lodge meets on the first and the third Thursday of every month at 8:00pm, (during degree nights we meet at 7:00pm). Our Values


MISSION: The Most Worshipful Prince Hall Grand Lodge of Free and Accepted Masons. State of California Inc. is a Mutual Benefit nonprofit organization which embodies a beautiful system of morality, veiled in allegory and illustrated by symbols. The Fraternity is founded upon Christian principles established in the Holy Bible strives to teach a man the duty he owes to God, his neighbor, and to himself; but, interfere either with religion nor politics as it prescribes the practice of virtues in the conduct of its business. Its foundation is CHARACTER. Its purpose is SERVICE. Its measure is GIVING. Its foundation is perpetuated by making “GOOD MEN BETTER.” Its purpose is service, which is rendered to the people of California to improve their social, cultural, and economic condition, Its measure is giving, an act of unselfish sacrifice for the benefit of others. This Mission Statement is promulgated by the Most Worshipful Prince Hall Grand Lodge and the subordinate Lodges owing obedience to the same, and by its Supreme Authority.


VISION: Our vision is paint the world with the theme of benevolent peace and universal humanity. As the clinche goes "when you know better you do better'; thus, Western Knights Lodge #56 vision is make the world a better place loking on the bright side of optimism, positivity and have fun through social intervourse / brotherhood
